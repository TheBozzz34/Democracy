Checks That Balance

The judicial branch would have more power over congress in order to more effectivly balance the three branches of goverment.

Stage Two

The top of the political system, like in our current democracy, would consist of a government at the federal level. This would continue to have our current three branches of government working in much the same way as they do now with the addition of another piece of government which would be our version of the tribunal system. This would be a part of the judicial branch which would hold veto power over the other two branches. The purpose of the Tribunes would be to have the interests of marginalized people such as the poor and minority groups best represented to try to make elections as fair as possible.
